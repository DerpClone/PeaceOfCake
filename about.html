About Us

PeaceOfCake.com is a newly launched website under our company of PeaceOfCake. The main goal of this website is to provide an online platform for catering and delivery services to our beloved patrons, as well as the sharing of free recipes of pastries to all sweet toothed lovers and baking enthusiasists! We hope to spread our love in our freshly made goods through the site and build a positive image on the controlled indulgence of delicious pastries. Our goods are made fresh and with love from our wholesome team here at PeaceOfCake!
